INFORMATION:

- What is Sapphire State?

Sapphire State has been around since 2022 and has been built by the community. It's a dynamic and immersive roleplay server using the QBCore framework within FiveM that uses the GTA V game's AI and network code.

We are dedicated to providing a balanced mix of realism and creativity, ensuring that your roleplay story never stops and never gets boring offering a wide range of interactions.

Step into a city alive with possibilities. Whether you're a seasoned roleplayer or new to the scene, Sapphire State offers endless opportunities to create and develop your character's journey. From criminal empires and law enforcement careers to business ownership and everyday civilian life, there's always something new to explore.

No matter what role you choose, Sapphire State ensures your story is always evolving. Whether you're building an empire, fighting crime, or simply living the city life, every choice you make shapes the world around you.

Join Sapphire State today and carve out your legacy!

- What makes Sapphire State unique?

At Sapphire State, we go beyond the basics of roleplay to bring you a living, breathing world where every action matters. We offer a wide variety of immersive roleplay opportunities with a focus on dynamic storytelling, player-driven economy, and meaningful interactions.

Join the ranks of criminals and rise through the underworld using strategy, skill, and teamwork. With over 10+ criminal activities, the path you take is up to you—whether it's running an underground empire, pulling off high-stakes heists, or making your name in the black market. Crime in Sapphire State isn't just about money—it's about power, reputation, and control. Will you rule the streets or go down in flames?

Not everyone chooses a life of crime. Sapphire State offers a fully developed civilian economy with over 10+ jobs, each with progression and unique gameplay mechanics. Start as a blue-collar worker, climb the corporate ladder, or launch your own business—your story is in your hands. From real estate and car dealerships to bartending and construction, the city is full of opportunities to make a name for yourself.
